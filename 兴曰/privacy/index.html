隐私政策

生效日期：2026 年 7 月 18 日

兴曰尊重您的隐私。应用以本地处理为核心，不运营用于收集个人使用数据的服务器。

数据收集

我们不会收集、上传或出售您的个人信息、收藏记录、浏览记录或通知偏好。

本地存储

您收藏的语录、当前阅读位置、背景选择和通知设置均保存在您的设备本地。删除应用可能会同时删除这些本地数据。

通知权限

仅当您启用语录提醒时，兴曰才会请求通知权限。通知由系统在设备端安排；您可以随时在 iOS“设置”中关闭权限。

内容来源

应用随安装包提供整理后的公开语录及来源链接。打开来源链接时，您将离开兴曰并受相应网站的隐私政策约束。

第三方服务

兴曰未集成第三方广告、分析或行为追踪 SDK。

儿童隐私

我们不会有意收集任何年龄用户的个人数据，包括儿童的个人数据。

政策更新

如本政策发生变化，我们会更新本页面的生效日期。

联系我们

如有隐私相关问题，请联系 madfool@icloud.com。

Effective: July 18, 2026

Xing Said respects your privacy. The app is built around on-device processing and does not operate servers that collect personal usage data.

Data collection

We do not collect, upload, or sell your personal information, favorites, reading history, or notification preferences.

Local storage

Your favorites, current reading position, background choice, and notification settings are stored locally on your device. Removing the app may also remove this local data.

Notifications

Xing Said requests notification permission only if you enable quote reminders. Reminders are scheduled on your device, and permission can be disabled at any time in iOS Settings.

Content sources

The app bundle includes compiled public quotes and source links. Opening a source link takes you outside Xing Said, where the destination website's privacy policy applies.

Third parties

Xing Said does not include third-party advertising, analytics, or behavioral tracking SDKs.

Children

We do not knowingly collect personal data from users of any age, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the effective date shown on this page.

Contact

For privacy questions, email madfool@icloud.com.
